Phani's Bio

I was born and brought up in India. After completing my Bachelors' in Civil Engineering at IIT Madras, I got a scholarship to complete my Masters' in Transportation at MIT. After that I moved to Dallas in 2004, I started working at Wilbur Smith Associates (now CDM Smith) where I did travel demand modeling and forecasting for several toll roads in Texas, Florida and Colorado. Over the years I have led and managed forecasting studies that helped with the financing/refinancing of more than $15B in toll revenue bonds. I have mentored and trained several professionals and built high-performing teams to advance road infrastructure programs for several government agencies. I currently work as a Senior Director at WSP USA in Dallas, TX. I am a civil engineer by profession, but have over the years developed interest in the following:

  1. Travel Demand Modeling/Forecasting
  2. Data analytics/Coding
  3. Catan
